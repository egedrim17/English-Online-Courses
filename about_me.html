My name is Evelina Gedrimaite and here is my story.

One year before entering the university I was a regular teenager studying in a regular Russian school. I barely spoke any English. My grammar skills were below the average as well. However, once I decided to study at LCC International University and it motivated me to improve my English so I started working on it very hard. In less than 6 month I passed TOEFL exam and got accepted to LCC International Univeristy located in Klaipeda, Lithuania.

Right now I am a third year student of LCC International University, Comtemporary Communication Major.

Within these 3 years I have visited 3 continents: Europe (United Kingdom, Germany, Poland and France), North America (USA) and Africa (Tanzania)

Moreover, I had the opportunity to visit London once again and spend 3 monthes living and working in there (summer 2018). Also, I during the summer 2019 I participated in "Work and Travel" program visiting the United States, New York state.

Knowledge of English turned my life over and opened new horizons for me. It became much more easier to travel and to communicate with different cultures getting some new knowledge.
